Response on Media Queries

Media Queries allows you to change the appearance of the design according to certai variables. Such as the screen size. You can have the media querie be able to change the color of the text, background color, even the text size when the size of the screen changes. This would then be a responsive design, where the design would respond to the size of the screen and respond with changes to the design.

Our problem was that there wasnt a good way to view the stats of the players playing in a game at our school. There would be some sites or apps but, they wouldnt be updating very fast. Our solution was to make an app called WESPN, which stood for Waipahu Entertainment Sports Programming Network. WESPN would take the stats of the players and upload it for the poeple to see, being able to see play by plays along with the scores. I would improve my video by taking more shots of us to find a better one instead of just taking one shot and being "done". Also we should have planned out the video better d=so that we could get better quality shots according to the lighting and phone availability. Our features would include play by play actions, stats of the jv and varsity teams, stats of each indvidual players. One feature I would like to add are pictures of our players next to their names, so that the people would know wh they're looking at. We didn't really work out much of a schedule for us to record our videos. Also some of us never contribute as much as others. What I would do differently next time would be to plan out our schedule for filming. Also to have everyone contribute into our video.
